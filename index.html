About K.R. Mangalam University

Established in 2013, K.R. Mangalam University is a premier institution located in Gurugram, Haryana. With a sprawling 30-acre campus, we are dedicated to providing a holistic education that blends academic rigor with practical industry experience.

Our university is recognized by UGC and accredited with an NAAC A Grade. We foster an environment of innovation, research, and global collaboration.

Research And Innovation

The university encourages interdisciplinary research, innovation, and entrepreneurship among students and faculty.

  • Innovation Labs: Dedicated spaces for prototyping and AI/ML research.
  • Startup Incubation: Support for student-led startups.
  • Collaborations: Partnerships with IITs, NITs, and global universities.
Research Centre

The Research Centre serves as a hub for faculty and Ph.D. scholars to conduct high-impact research in Engineering, Sciences, Management, and Humanities.

  • Funded Projects: DST, UGC, and AICTE sponsored projects.
  • Publications: Scopus, WoS, and Peer-Reviewed journals.
  • Workshops: Regular sessions on research methodology and data science.
