About K.R. Mangalam University

Established in 2013, K.R. Mangalam University is a premier institution located in Gurugram, Haryana. With a sprawling 30-acre campus, we are dedicated to providing a holistic education that blends academic rigor with practical industry experience.

Our university is recognized by UGC and accredited with an NAAC A Grade. We foster an environment of innovation, research, and global collaboration.
